    An identity system for SAIC's Spring Architecture Lecture Series.

    This hypothetical lecture series invites world renowned architects to the SAIC campus to celebrate and learn from their work and achievement. Since SAIC is known for it's interdisciplinary curriculum, I drew inspiration for the central illustration from the architectural forms and textures of wheel thrown ceramics. The muted color palette and stacked type construction echoes the architectural landscape of Chicago.
