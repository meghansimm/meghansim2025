    A biographical publication about the life and work of artist Cindy Sherman.

    Sherman's work is beautiful, frightening, and undeniably compelling. Uncomfortable typefaces, margins, and shifts in scale are incorporated to compliment this philosophy. The few printed copies are hand-bound using an experimental perfect binding technique which keeps keeps prints intact across the gutter. This was specifically developed to properly present Sherman's Centerfolds (1981).
